## Production Brief

**Situation:** A mother of two boys facing serial eviction in Milwaukee. Each eviction triggers a cascade — new school, lost job, broken social connections, deeper poverty. The eviction record itself becomes a barrier to future housing.

**Challenge/Opportunity:** The dome must address the eviction cascade — how one housing loss triggers failures across employment, education, healthcare, and social connection. The financial instruments need to make coordination cheaper than fragmentation.

**Key Systems:** housing court, rental assistance, public housing, TANF, Medicaid, public schools, employment services

**Flourishing Dimensions:** housing, employment, education, community, dignity, stability, financial security

**Ryan Coogler**
