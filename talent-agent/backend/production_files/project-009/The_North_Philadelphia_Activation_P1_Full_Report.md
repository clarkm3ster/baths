## Production Brief

**Situation:** Vacant since 2008. Was a gas station, then briefly a used car lot. Surrounded by institutions — Temple University nearby, community health centers, churches. An institutional neighborhood with a hole in its center.

**Challenge/Opportunity:** Positioned between institutional anchors. The activation could connect what's already there — a public square, a market, a performance space that serves the university AND the long-term residents. The site's emptiness is a communication failure between institutions that are 2 blocks apart.

**Zoning:** CMX-3 (Commercial Mixed Use)
**Lot Size:** 15,000 sqft
**Constraints:** Previous gas station requires environmental study, High traffic requires careful pedestrian design, Community benefit agreement should precede development

**James Corner / Field Operations**
